About Aperus

Built on real-world Microsoft expertise

Aperus Ltd is a UK-based technology consultancy incorporated in September 2022, specialising in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Customer Engagement and bespoke mobile application development.

Founded by a practising Dynamics 365 CE consultant with hands-on experience across Sales, Customer Service, and Marketing modules, Aperus bridges the gap between enterprise CRM capability and modern mobile-first experiences. We build iOS and Android applications that connect directly to the Microsoft ecosystem — giving clients a seamless path from back-office CRM to customer-facing app.

We work with UK businesses of all sizes, delivering pragmatic, well-engineered solutions without the overhead of a large consultancy.
